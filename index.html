Answers to common questions about our services.

We groom a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, rabbits, and other small animals. Our groomers are experienced with all breeds and sizes!

Generally, it's recommended to groom dogs every 4-6 weeks and cats every 6-8 weeks. However, the frequency may vary based on your pet's breed, coat type, and lifestyle.

Yes, we use only high-quality, pet-friendly grooming products that are safe and gentle on your pet's skin and coat.
